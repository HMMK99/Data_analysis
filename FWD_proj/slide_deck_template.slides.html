The relations between Prosper Rating and other parameters¶

by Hatem Mohammed Kamal¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to know the relation between Prosper Rating categories and some other variables in the data set.

Dataset Overview¶

a 113937 rows * 81 columns data about loans and the details related to borrower and the loan itself.
